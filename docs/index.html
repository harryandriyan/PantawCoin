Frequently Asked Questions

How does PantawCoin get cryptocurrency data?

PantawCoin uses the CoinGecko API to fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices and information.

How often are prices updated?

Prices are automatically refreshed every 5 minutes. You can also manually refresh at any time by clicking the "Refresh Prices" option.

Can I add cryptocurrencies that aren't in the default list?

Yes! You can add custom cryptocurrencies by selecting "Add Custom Coin" from the menu and entering the ticker symbol.

How do I set which cryptocurrency appears in my menu bar?

Click on any cryptocurrency in your favorites list and select "Set as Primary Display" from the submenu.

Is PantawCoin free?

Yes, PantawCoin is completely free and open-source under the MIT License.
